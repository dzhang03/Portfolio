Danny

SMASH@Wharton scholar, Danny Zhang, exemplifies social justice and esprit de corps on campus while also modeling the highest academic standards in the classroom. “I love to make people feel like they’re worth something and make them feel like family when they’re around me. I treat everyone with respect and they’re all my brothers and sisters.” Danny particularly enjoys peer learning, “I enjoy learning and being able to spread my knowledge to other people. It makes me smile to see others learn from me because it comes to show that I’m able to learn and they’re able to learn while I teach.”

Danny is a rising sophomore at Parkway Center City Middle College High School and plans to attend Community College of Philadelphia in his sophomore year and forward. Danny is also an athlete studying dance, playing football and basketball.
